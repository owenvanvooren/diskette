what’s diskette?

i started diskette as a small design lab focused on fun and intuitive user interfaces, unique identities, and outside-the-box thinking. behind diskette is me, owen van vooren, a designer who loves turning ideas both mundane and extraordinary into wonderful visuals with personality and purpose. i work closely with clients to create a polished final product that feels intentional, modern, and uniquely theirs.
