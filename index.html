design beliefs

i believe design should be approachable, not intimidating. the best interfaces feel natural, intuitive, and delightful. every small microinteraction and animation can have a profound impact on the way people experience a product, service, or tool.
fun, playful, and quirky interfaces resonate with people better than a generic, lifeless designs. creating an effective, memorable, and seamless experience involves making people feel something wonderful.
